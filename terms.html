alfieos is a personal automation tool built and operated by its developer for their own personal use. It is not a public service and does not accept sign-ups from other users.

What it does

alfieos uses TikTok's Content Posting API to upload videos, created by its developer, as drafts to the developer's own TikTok account inbox. The developer then manually reviews and publishes each draft from within the TikTok app.

Scope

This application only accesses and posts to the TikTok account of its own developer. It does not act on behalf of, or access data belonging to, any other TikTok user.
